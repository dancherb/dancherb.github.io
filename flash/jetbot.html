2012. A simple yet increasingly challenging puzzle game about lighting a path through hexagons. New tiles are introduced throughout the game, but the aim was to keep the focus on mastering simple and fair mechanics, slowly introducing extremely difficult challenges, and leaving it to you to figure out what to do. Comes with a level editor for creating and sharing tiles - also, try switching the theme from the settings menu for a change of atmosphere. Featured on the front page of Newgrounds, where it also won daily feature. Sponsored by Spiked Math Games, with licenses also being purchased by ArmorGames and CoolMathGames. Music by Morgan McSweeney.
